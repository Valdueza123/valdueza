Music is an important part of our life as it is a way of expressing our feelings as well as emotions. Some people consider music as a way to escape from the pain of life. It gives you relief and allows you to reduce the stress.


Growing up, I have listened to many types and genres of music, exploring what I like and what I don't. Listening to music is one of my ways to relax and kill some time. I usually listen to music when I am bored or just want to sing along to the lyrics. I always listen to music when I'm on my way to something like school or to any of my destinations, especially when commuting. It is my way of enjoying things in life and relaxing my body and mind while doing something or waiting for something.
