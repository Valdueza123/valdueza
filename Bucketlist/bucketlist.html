     I love historic or historical places thats why i want to visit Intramuros. Intramuros is a historic walled area located in Manila, Philippines, and there are many reasons why people choose to visit this unique and fascinating destination. Intramuros is one of the oldest parts of Manila and has a rich history dating back to the Spanish colonial era. Visitors can explore the historic walls, fortifications, and buildings that have been preserved and restored to their original state. This area is a living museum that provides a glimpse into Manila's colonial past. Intramuros has a unique architectural style that reflects its Spanish colonial past. Visitors can see examples of Baroque and Renaissance-style architecture, including the Manila Cathedral and San Agustin Church. Intramuros hosts many cultural events throughout the year, including art exhibits, music festivals, and historical reenactments. Visitors can experience the rich culture and heritage of the Philippines through these events.






     Boracay is one of the least places I want to visit but nevertheless i still want to experience going here. Boracay is a small island located in the Western Visayas region of the Philippines, and there are many reasons why people choose to visit this beautiful and popular destination. Boracay is known for its stunning white sand beaches that are among the best in the world. Visitors can relax on the soft sand, swim in the crystal-clear waters, and soak up the sun. Boracay offers a wide range of water sports activities, including scuba diving, snorkeling, windsurfing, kiteboarding, and parasailing. The island's clear waters and abundant marine life make it a popular destination for water sports enthusiasts.
